Uncompromising Professionalism – Dr. Z. Schuber, A Different Level of Service.

Dr. Z. Schuber has many years of experience in the field of dentures, focusing on the precise fitting of dentures to significantly improve oral function and aesthetic appearance.

Over the years, many patients who have used dentures for a long time experience bone resorption in the jaw and gums. This process causes a decrease in facial height, impairs the ability to chew and speak, and sometimes creates an older appearance.

1 Stage One – Restoring Height and Function

In this stage, we adjust and restore the old dentures, restoring the correct vertical dimension and proper muscle tone. This process improves eating, speaking, and expression, and restores facial harmony. The old denture serves as a temporary one for about two weeks.

2 Stage Two – Crafting New, Perfectly Fitted Dentures

After the muscles and jaws return to normal function, we produce new dentures. Thanks to the precise preparation, adaptation is faster and easier — without the need for repeated adjustments.

  • Improved eating and speaking function
  • A younger, more harmonious facial appearance
  • Maximum comfort and a custom fit

Old dentures can cause instability while eating, difficulty speaking, and daily discomfort. We are here to change that.

Denture Relining

Over the years, there is a natural decrease in the amount of bone that supports the denture, which can cause instability and a poorer grip. In this situation, we perform a renewal of the inner part of the denture — to allow for a more perfect fit to the oral cavity. This improves grip and comfort, without making a new denture.

Denture Cleaning

Just like on natural teeth, tartar can accumulate on a denture over time. This can be prevented with proper maintenance and daily cleaning of the denture. If tartar has already accumulated, we take care to remove it gently and efficiently using dedicated mechanical means, keeping your denture clean, hygienic, and pleasant to use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an extra charge for the at-home service?

Our service is designed to provide maximum convenience. The cost of treatment includes the visit to your home, without surprises or hidden fees for the home service.

Who is the at-home treatment suitable for?

The service is ideal for seniors, people with limited mobility, or anyone who prefers to save travel and waiting time at the clinic and receive personal, quiet care in their familiar environment.

How long does the two-stage process take?

The first stage of rehabilitating the existing dentures takes about two weeks. After function and vertical dimension stabilize, we proceed to create the new dentures, a process that takes several more appointments for a perfect fit.

Do the dentures look natural?

Absolutely. Dr. Schuber places great emphasis on the aesthetic appearance and facial harmony. We restore the facial height and support the lip and cheek muscles for a younger, more natural look.

What if my denture loses its stability?

In such cases, we perform a "relining" – renewing the inner part of the denture and adapting it to the current gum structure. This is a quick solution that significantly improves grip and comfort without replacing the entire denture.

What materials do you use to make the dentures?

We use some of the highest quality materials on the market, ensuring maximum durability and comfort. In addition, we integrate a cast mesh or reinforcing quartz within the denture to prevent future fractures.

How is the dentures price determined?

The dentures price depends on several factors, such as the type of denture (partial or complete), the materials used, and whether preliminary treatments are required. We offer high-quality, professional at-home care at fair prices with no surprises.

Do you also handle cases of fixed dentures?

We provide a wide range of oral rehabilitation solutions, including consultation and fitting for fixed dentures (implant-supported) or premium removable dentures, depending on the patient's clinical condition and needs.
